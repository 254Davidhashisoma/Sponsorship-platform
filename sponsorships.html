Moringa School is a coding school that provides technology and professional training to students, through intensive rapid skills training programs. Moringa is developing the next generation of world-class software developers through market-driven education. The school currently offers a ready-to-work model, training students in vocational IT skills (Moringa offers classes in software development and data science) and soft skills (e.g., leadership and teamwork) to prepare them to work effectively.
